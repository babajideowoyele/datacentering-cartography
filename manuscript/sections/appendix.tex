\section{Facility Index Schema}
\label{app:schema}
% ============================================================

The facility index is the shared reference dataset linking all four work packages.
\Cref{tab:schema} describes each field. The index is maintained as a community-editable
CSV at \texttt{data/facility\_index/facilities.csv} (facility-level data) and
\texttt{scripts/wp1/config\_template.csv} (operator-level scraping configuration).
Contributing guidelines are documented in \texttt{CONTRIBUTING.md}.

\section{WP1 Keyword Vocabulary and ECI Weighting Scheme}
\label{app:vocabulary}
% ============================================================

The ECI is a weighted sum of keyword category frequencies, normalised by token count.
\Cref{tab:eci} documents the full vocabulary and weights used in the seed run.
Weights are provisional and will be calibrated against a labelled validation sample
in the full pipeline run. The vocabulary is case-insensitive and applied after stopword
removal; multi-word phrases are matched before single-word fallback.
